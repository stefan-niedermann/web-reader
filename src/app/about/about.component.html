About Web Reader

Web Reader allows you to let your browser read all kind of content for you. The results depend on the operating system and browser you are using.

You can (and should) set the language of the reader voice to match the language of the inserted text. Press the gear next to the "Play"-button to change the voice. You can also find an option to level the speed
